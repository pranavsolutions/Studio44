ClariGlow Facial

Facial for Oily Skin - Oily skin is caused by over-active sebaceous glands. Oily skin is characterized by shine and enlarged pores. On account of the high levels of sebum, the skin has a greasy shine and tendency to break out into acne. This is because oily skin tends to attract more dirt and dust. The benefits of oily skin and enlarged pore treatment are well worth the time and effort.

- Reduces and controls imperfections on oily skin
- Enhances natural glow while leaving the skin with matte appearance

Steps

Step 1 – ClariWash
- Contains Tea Tree Oil that has antibacterial and anti-fungal properties. Also contains Lemon Extract that clears excessive sebum oil on the skin. It exfoliates dead skin cells that prevent the accumulation of pore clogging impurities. Niacinamide that regulates sebum and reduces oily shine. Gluconodeltalactone acts as a mild exfoliator. It also lightens and moisturizes the skin to provide even skin tone. Salicylic Acid has exfoliating action, sweeping away dead cells. Oat Amino Acid reduces skin irritation and excessive sebum.
- Apply the entire contents of the sachet of Cheryl’s ClariWash™ on the neck and face and massage with circular anti-clockwise movements for three minutes. Moisten hand with water for smooth massage movements. After cleansing, wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 2 – ClariBuf
- Contains Oats Extract that acts as an antiirritant and anti-inflammatory agent.
- Also contains BHA that dissolves excess oil, gently exfoliating it from the skin.
- Apply the contents of the sachet containting Cheryl’s ClariBuf™ on the neck and face and massage for 3 minutes with steam and another 3 minutes without steam. Then wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 3 – ClariGel
- Contains Biosulphur Fluid that acts as a soothing agent. Sodium Bicarbonate that softens blackheads, white heads and comedones.
- Apply the entire content of sachet containting Cheryl’s ClariGel™ on the area with blackheads and give Galvanic negative (‒) polarity current for 5 – 7 minutes. Proceed with extractions using cotton buds as demonstrated. Then wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 4 – ClariSoothe
- Contains A C NET that inhibits the excess sebum production. Urea that hydrates and softens the skin. Glycerin acts as a humectant. Silicone Oil imparts emollcency to the skin.
- Apply the contents of the sachet on the neck and face and massage for 10 minutes, using Cheryl’s signature massage movements. Then wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 5 – ClariMask
- Contains Comfrey Root Extract that improves skin tone. Zinc Oxide and Titanium Dioxide that provides complete UV protection and have anti-inflammatory properties. Salicylic Acid has an exfoliating action, sweeping away excessive build up of dead cells. Tea Tree is an antimicrobial agent. Niacinamide regulates sebum, reducing oily shine. A C NET inhibits the excess sebum production.
- Apply the contents of the sachet on the neck and face with a Cheryl’s pack brush and leave on for 15 minutes. Then remove the mask and clean the face the with a mummy mask as demonstrated.

Step 6 – ClariTone
- Contains Zinc PCA that regulates the sebum production. Allantoin than exfoliates layers of dead skin cells, thereby increasing the smoothness of the skin. Cucumber Extract that constricts pores. It also cools, soothes and hydrates the skin. Bisabolol has anti-inflammatory and anti-bacterial properties. Polysaccharide 5 from sea, gives immediate mattifying effect, reducing the shine.
- Apply the contents of Cheryl’s ClariTone™ on the face & neck, pat uniformly into the skin and leave on.
