OxyBlast Facial

Oxygen Energising Facial - This facial replenishes lost oxygen and the active ingredients work to restructure the skin. It visibly fills up the lines and wrinkles and stimulates cell regeneration. Delivers oxygen deep into the skin where skin health, elasticity and firmness begin, leading to increased luminosity and enhanced radiance. This facial lightens and hydrates skin and reverses the damage caused by free radicals making it the perfect facial for dull and damaged skin that needs radiance and energizing.

- Rejuvenates dry, mature and dull skin
- Lightens & hydrates skin
- Reduces fine lines & wrinkles

Steps

Step 1 – Cleansing Gel
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Cleansing Gel sachet all over face and neck. - Massage gently in circular movement. - Wipe off with damp cotton.

Step 2 – Deep Peel
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Deep Peel sachet all over face and neck. - Massage for 2 minutes with steam and for 3 minutes without steam. - Wipe off with damp cotton.

Step 3 – Skin Oxygenator
- Pour the entire content of OxyBlast™ Skin Oxygenator sachet on cotton and apply all over face.

Step 4 – Rejuvenating Serum
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Rejuvenating Serum sachet all over face and neck. - Massage gently and leave on.

Step 5 – Deep Penetrating Mask
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Deep Penetrating Mask sachet on face.
- Place cling film on face (with cut-out for nose) and steam skin for 3 to 5 minutes.
- Leave on for 10 to 15 minutes and wipe off with damp cotton.

Step 6 – Energising Cream
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Energising Cream sachet on face and neck.
- Gently massage into skin with light upward strokes.
- Dip hands in water or fruit juice to facilitate facial massage.

Step 7 – Soothing Mask
- Apply entire content of OxyBlast™ Soothing Mask sachet all over face and neck, avoiding the eyes.
- Leave on for 8 to 10 minutes and wipe off with damp cotton.
