O2C2 Facial

Radiance Facial - Radiant skin is the outward sign of the youth and energy they feel inside, but even minor sun damage can cause skin to become dull and lackluster.
- Helps decrease premature ageing effects of sun damage and free radicals.
- Increases skin moisture and hydration.
- Enhances skin elasticity.
- Nourishes the deeper layers of the skin.
- Help maintain integrity and structure of the skin

.

Steps

Step 1
- O2C2™ Deep Cleanser contains Niacinamide that improves skin’s elasticity and helps reduce discoloration. Ferulic acid reduces melanin in the skin. Salicylic acid exfoliates dead skin cells. Green tea is a powerful anti-oxidant.
- Empty entire content of the sachet containting O2C2™ Deep Cleanser in a glass bowl and mix with little water, till the powder mixes thoroughly. Apply it over wet neck and face with a treatment brush and then massage in small circular movements for 3 minutes. For smooth massage movements, add a few drops of water in the glass bowl, work up rich foam, apply on the face and continue massaging. After cleansing, wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 2
- O2C2™ Freshner contains Allantoin that soothes the skin. Also contains Zinc PCA that reduces sebum secretion. Cucumber extract constricts pores, cools and hydrates the skin. Sodium PCA absorbs and retains moisture. Lactic acid reduces melanin in the skin.
- Apply the entire content of the sachet containting O2C2™ Oxygenating Freshner on moist cotton and tone the neck and face with gentle tapping movements. Leave on and do not wipe off.

Step 3
- O2C2™ Crean contains MSM that reduces inflammation. Also contains Almind oil which is an excellent emollient. Shea butter hydrates the skin and promotes regeneration of cells. Lactic acid has anti-bacterial properties and also hydrates the skin. Perfluorodecalin transfers oxygen into the skin.
- Apply the entire contents of the sachet containting O2C2™ Oxygenating Cream on the neck and face and massage, using Signature Massage technique for 10 minutes. Moisten hands with water for smooth massage movements. Wipe clean with moist cotton.

Step 4A
- O2C2™ Peel Activator contains Papain and Bromelain, proteolytic enzymes that digest dead skin. Also contains Citric acid that promotes younger looking skin by increasing the rate of cell renewal. Sodium bicarbonate reduces excessive oily secretions.
- Break open one O2C2™ Peel Acticator capsule and empty the entire contents into the enclosed container. Fill container half way with warm water and mix well. Wait for 1 minute for a gel to form (the gel may be lumpy in consistence). Now open the sachet containing O2C2™ Peel Activator – 1 & add the entire contents into the mixing container containting the gel formed. Mix the product well, apply a thick layer of the enzyme gel to the entire neck & face with Cheryl’s treatment brush. Leave for 3 min & donot wipe off.

Step 4B
- O2C2™ Peeling cream contains Stavilized oxygen that provides anti-bacterial and anti-inflammatory peeling. Also contains Vitamin C, a powerful anti-oxidant that lightens the skin.
- Vitamin E acts as a strong anti-oxidant that also reduces fine lines and wrinkles.
- Apply the entire content of the sachet containing O2C2™ Peeling Crean on top of the gel. Gently massage for 1 minute and then leave for 3-5 minutes. Remove the oxygen foam by rinsing off (if skin is sensitive) or by using the enclosed spatula in a shaving motion. Rinse thoroughly with room temperature or lukewarm water. Blot the skin with a paper tissue.

Step 5
- Zinc Oxide and Micronized Titanium dioxide provide complete UV protection and have anti-inflammatory properties. Bisoctrizole and Bemotrizinol are patented UV filters. Olive leaf extract prevents sun-damage with anti-oxidant propeties.
- Apply the contents of the sachet containting SPF15 Spray on the neck and face with gentle patting movements.
