Wedding FAQ's

What are the Salon Services you recommend for a groom??

Generally we recommend services in terms of Hair Styling, Hair Care, Skin Care and Makeup, based on the Skin Type and Condition of a particular groom. Could range from a simple Hair Cut or a Change in Hair Style to Treatments for Hair Fall or Dandruff to Grey Hair Colouring. For skin, a simple De-Tan or Cleanup to Advanced Facial Treatments. It is also highly depends on the time period a groom gives us before his marriage.

When should I start my skin care regime for my wedding?

The ideal time is now, if your wedding is just a week from now or still a 6 months to go. The earlier the better. The longer the period you avail the Pre-wedding Grooming routine, the better.

What do you mean by Pre-wedding Grooming Routine?

It's a series of unique Salon Services and do-it-yourself at-Home skin care services, recommended to you. And it includes timing the Hair Cuts and Beard, so your hair styles the way you prefer on your Wedding Day.

How long each facial session takes?

It should be less than an hour, depending on the facial type.

What do you do in a facial?

The common steps in a facial are Deep Cleansing, Toning, Exfoliation with Scrub, Steam and Comedone Extraction, Mask Application, Facial Massage, Moisturisation and Sun-Protection.

I have Black-heads and White-heads on my nose and cheeks.

Black-heads and White-heads are technically called as Comedones. There's a step in facial where after steam, they're extracted away.

When should I get a facial last before my wedding day?

A week to 3 days before the wedding day is recommended.

Will I get the results of a facial only after 3 days?

Results are instant. You don't have to wait for 3 or more days to see the facial's effects. Follow the Home care tips provided by your beautician, for lasting results.

Will there be any side effects?

If you've ever been allergic to any sunscreen lotion or any other skin care cosmetics earlier you used, inform the beautician about it. He can suggest you alternative solutions. In an extremely rare chance, you may be allergic to any of the ingredients used in facial. In that case, we take care of it by applying ice cubes to ease the condition.

Why should I get a facial for my wedding?

As your wedding will be an important day in your life and there'll be lots of eyeballs on you. The cameras photographers’ use these days being high-res, it catches every minute detail of you. Your face being the only skin you show, no reason why you shouldn't make it look natural. As many of the friends and relatives of your in-laws, who look at you for the first time, judge by your appearance. As the old saying goes, "First impression is the best impression." The reason to look good on your wedding is more political than natural. Decision is yours.

My younger brother too wants to get a facial.

If he's above 21 years of age, he can. (Teenagers, ask your mom. Her home-made skin care recipe is just fine.)

I want facials for my best-men / best friends.

Welcome. You're a true friend.

Other Salons provide Gold facial / Herbal facial / Fruit facial. Do you?

Ya, we noticed. A lot bigger Salon Chains to next-door barber shops do claim they provide gold / herbal / fruit facials. At Rs. 25000+ per 10 gram, we wonder how one can add gold as ingredient in a facial. Fishy, right! Gold, is a metal which won't react with your skin at all. For herbal and fruit, once it's in a bottle, it's no more a herbal or fruit. There should be preservatives and flavoring agents added. Check the ingredients if it's only herbal or fruit. But actual fresh fruits can be used as a good face mask, which you can do-it-yourself at home. Ask our beautician for guidance.

I have grey hair only on the front. Will you color only on there?

We recommend you go for a global hair color of your natural shade. We use Ammonia-free Professional color which doesn't damage your hair, unlike cheap dyes and fast colors. Our color lasts a minimum of 24 shampoo washes.

What is difference between a facial and a makeup?

A facial is an in-salon skin care treatment process to restore your natural skin tone and condition. The results of facial are lasting. Makeup is application of skin-toned cream to make your skin look even and natural. Makeup lasts till you wash and has no skin care value.

Why I need an anti-Hair fall treatment?

Hair fall is part of hair's growth cycle. It's quiet natural even if there's 50+ hairs when you wash. Every hair matures and falls. After a brief break, hair grows from the same follicle. If you have healthy lifestyle practices and take good food, you needn't worry about hair fall. But if you see abnormal hair fall, you may have to take anti-hairfall measures. Take advice from our stylists when you visit us next time.

Why I need an anti-dandruff treatment?

You are single now and soon you are gonna share life with a partner. Also you are gonna share material things, like pillow, towel, comb, anything and everything. Managing dandruff for men is super easy as you have a shorter hair. But in your wife's case, if you have infected her with your dandruff, managing it will be hell for her. So consider, go clean-up before your marriage.

Makeup session for Muhurtham too? Or only for the reception?

If you've had a couple of facial and home care well before your marriage, you may not even need a makeup, as you'll look naturally good. But if you insist, you may go for makeup for your reception function, which will have a well-lit stage and a longer photo session.

Which makeup products you use?

We use Kryolan, which we find is more natural looking and gives a no-makeup look.

What do you do in makeup?

Makeup involves: Cleansing, Deep Cleansing, Prepping, Concealing, Foundation, Setting, Hair Styling and Setting.

Will it look patchy if I sweat?

No. Products we use in Kryolan are pigment based, which are water-proof. Tap away your sweat with a clean cotton hanky or a soft tissue.

Will the facial make me fair? Will you get me 'Aarae vaarangalil sigappalagu'?

A big NO. We can't make you any fairer toned than you actually are. We jus can help you restore your natural skin tone and condition.

What are my home care?

Generally, Wash your face twice daily with a good face wash. Plenty available in the market, exclusively for men. Find the suitable one for you. Use a good Moisturizer and Sun-protection cream. If it's a both-in-one, awesome.

When should I get my last haircut before my wedding?

10 to 15 days before your wedding date, so you'll be neither sharp nor overgrown.

I have dark circles around my eyes. What to do?

Dark Circles under eyes are caused by various reason. It's more of a symptom. Get good sleep. Eat good food. Avoid smoke and drinks. Get a healthy lifestyle. If nothing works out, there's under-eye dark circle treatment with us. Ask our beautician.
