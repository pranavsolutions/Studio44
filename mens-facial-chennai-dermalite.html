Dermalite Facial

Fairness Facial - Benefits of DermaLite® Fairness Treatment Kit works together like a system to give you visibly lighter, glowing skin. The fairness system follows a holistic approach towards ensuring healthy skin and long term skin lightening. Together, the products effectively brighten the complexion to give you even toned, lighter skin with a healthy, lasting radiance.

Steps

Step 1 – Face Wash
- DermaLite® Face Wash contains Chamomile that soothes the skin. Also contains Licorice extract that works along woth Niacinamide to inhibit melanin production. Yoghurt moisturizes the skin. Niacinamide increases skin barrier ptotection.
- Appy the contents of the sachet containting DermaLite® Face Wash on the neck and face. Massage with circular anti-clockwise movements for 3 minutes. If required, moisten hands with little water for smooth massage movements. Wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 2 – Deep Cleansing
- DermaLite® Deep Cleanser contains Green Tea and Ferulic acid that are anti-oxidants. Also contains Niacinamide that lighterns the skin and improves skin texture. Trehalose protects the skin and retains mointure in the skin.
- Empty entire content of the sachet containing DermaLite® Deep Cleanser in glass bowl and mix with little water, till the powder dessolves completely. Apply it over moist neck and face with a Treatment brush and then massage in small circular movements for 3 minutes. For smooth massage movements, add a few drops of water in the glass bowl, work up rich foam, apply on the face and continue massaging. After cleansing wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 3 – Freshing
- DermaLite® Fairness Freshner contains Sodium PCA that absorbs & retains moisture. Also contains Cucumber extract that constricts pores. It also cools, soothes and hydrates skin. Zinc PCA regulates sebum production. Bisabolol is anti-irritant, anti-bacterial and soothes the skin. Glutathione whitens the skin and also has anti-oxidant properties.
- Apply the contents of the sachet containing DermaLite® Fairness Freshner on moist cotton and tone the face and neck with gentle patting movements. Leave in and do not wipe off.

Step 4 – Peel
- DermaLite® Peel contains Lactic acid that helps in skin dequamation and exfoliation and brings out fairness by exposing the new cells. Also contains Niacinamide that improves skin’s elasticity and revives skin’s healty tone and texture.
- Wear gloves. Pour the contents of the syringe containing DermaLite® Peel in a glass bowl and apply it on the face and neck with a treatment brush, starting from the forehead, then jaw, neck, nose and lastly cheeks. Leave on for 10 minutes and do not wipe off.

Step 5 – Neutralizing
- DermaLite® Peel Neutralizer contains Green tea extract and Sodium bicarbonate that neutralizes the peel. Also contains Sodium isomerate which is a moisture magnet.
- Wear gloves. Pour the contents of the sachet containing DermaLite® Peel Neutralizer in a glass bowl and apply it over the face and neck with a treatment brush, stating from forehead, jaw, neck, nose and cheeks. Massage gently with little water for 3 minutes and wipe the face clean with moist cotton.

Step 6 – Face Massage
- DermaLite® Cream contains Phospholipids that maintain the condition of the skin. Caprylic Capric triglyceride decreases moisture loss from the skin, thus conditioning the skin. Neopentyl glycol diethylhexanote and Lecithin moisturise the skin.
- Apply the contents of the sachet containing DermaLite® Vitality Cream on the neck and face and continue applying till the cream melts into a silky oil to facilitate massage movements. Do not use water and massage for 10 minutes using Signature Massage Techinque. After massage add water and massage gently till the cream turns milky. Wipe the face clean with moist cotton. The skin is moisturized without any oily residue.

Step 7 – Intensive Serum
- DermaLite® Intensive Concentrate contains Niacinamide that lightens the skin, Socium PCA absorbs & retains moisture.
- Pour the contents of the sachet containting DermaLite® Intensive Concentrate in a glass bowl and apply it on the neck and face with a treatment brush. Leave on for 5 minutes and do not wipe off.

Step 8 – Mask
- DermaLite® Fairness Mask contains Niacinamide lightens the skin and inhibits the production of melanin. Red algae gel hydrates the skin. Sodium hyaluronate hydrates and softens the skin.
- Remove DermaLite® Fairness Mask from the sachet and place it over the face. Spread it with hands or spatula and fit to face shape so the mask is tightly spread over the face. Leave on for 20 minutes. Then remove the mask, squeeze it to remove the excess residue and massage this residue into the neck and face till it is completely absorbed. Leave on and wash off after an hour.
